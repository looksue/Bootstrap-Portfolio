About Me

Sue is an aspiring full stack developer. She enjoys crafting websites that are user friendly and visually appealing.

Sue's hobbies are traveling, boating, snowmobiling, ATV'ing, camping, horticulture, baking, reading and learning new things.

Sue is originally from Upstate NY and recently moved to the Charlotte Area. She spent 16 years in education and prior to that 10 years in the travel industry.

Sue has a very large family. Her immediate family consists of her husband of 13 years and 4 daughters. She currently does not have any pets. She and her husband just became "empty-nesters".
